Frequently Asked Questions

Who is the event open to?

All current undergraduate and graduate students at the University of Tennessee, Knoxville and other schools in the University of Tennessee system are guaranteed spots. Current undergraduate and undergraduate students at other universities are encouraged to register and we will try to accomodate as many attendees as possible.

Will there be vegetarian, vegan, or gluten-free options for food?

Yes, we will have vegetarian and vegan options, and we will try to have gluten-free food as well. However, please indicate this when registering so we can best serve you.

Where do I sleep?

We will have a designated quiet sleeping area on site, and you are encouraged to bring sleeping bags, pillows, toiletries, and anything else you need to sleep. There are not showers at the venue, so please be prepared to go without showering unless you have other accomodations.

How much does it cost?

The event is completely free for eligible students (see first question above).

Where do I park?

We will distribute information to registred attendees prior to the event regarding where to park.
